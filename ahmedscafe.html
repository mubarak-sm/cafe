Welcome to Ahmeds Café!

You probably wonder why we call it a café.
That's simply because we believe this place emits the same energy:
A fresh space where you recharge, realign, and most importantly: EAT GOOD!

Ahmed used to treat his guests generously, going above and beyond. Inspired by this, we created our own tea with herbs that vary weekly and are given out for free. Although it's not always the best business move, we believe that doing the right thing pays off — in abundance. Therefore, first on the menu is TEA!


Whether you come on a casual Sunday, Tuesday evening or Friday night, you'll always be welcomed! Even though its impossible to achieve perfection, we still strive to achive greatness, this chase you can do even in the pursuit of dessert! Bon Apetit!
